Who are we

Syntax is a Official Coding Club of our college- a space where curious minds meet code. We're a team of students who love experimenting, breaking things, fixing them again, and learning in the process.

What We Do

From Coding Challenges and project building to fun tech events and workshops- we do it all! Our goal is simple: make coding less scary and more exciting to everyone, no matter your level.

Our Motto

Code. Connect. Collaborate. Learn.

  • Peer to Peer Learning- teaching each other is the best way to master Coding.
  • Learning by Doing- real projects, real challenges, real fun.
  • Team Spirit- we rise by lifting each other.
  • Innovation and curiosity- every idea deserves a chance to be built.

"When minds connect, great code happens."
